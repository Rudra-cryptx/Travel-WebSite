Day-wise Itinerary

Welcome to Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam! Upon arrival at Noi Bai International Airport, our representative will greet you and transfer you to your hotel for check-in.

Take some time to relax after your flight. In the evening, enjoy a special welcome candle-light dinner at a premium local restaurant.

  • Private airport transfer
  • Hotel check-in (Premium Room)
  • Exclusive candle-light welcome dinner

After breakfast, begin your exploration of this fascinating city. Visit the Ho Chi Minh Complex, including the Mausoleum and his stilt house.

Continue to the One Pillar Pagoda and the Temple of Literature, Vietnam's first university. In the afternoon, explore the Old Quarter by cyclo and end the day with a traditional Water Puppet Show.

  • Guided Hanoi City Tour
  • Cyclo ride through the Old Quarter
  • Tickets to the Water Puppet Show
  • Breakfast included

Leave Hanoi behind as you travel to the spectacular Ha Long Bay, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Board your traditional wooden junk boat for an overnight cruise through the limestone karsts.

Enjoy a fresh seafood lunch on board, visit stunning caves, go kayaking or swimming, and watch the sunset over the bay.

  • Scenic drive to Ha Long Bay
  • Overnight cruise on a luxury junk boat
  • Kayaking & cave exploration
  • All meals included on board

Wake up early for a Tai Chi session on the sundeck. Continue cruising and exploring before disembarking and returning to Hanoi for your flight to Da Nang.

Upon arrival in Da Nang, transfer to the charming ancient town of Hoi An for check-in.

  • Morning Tai Chi on board
  • Domestic flight to Da Nang
  • Transfer to Hoi An hotel
  • Breakfast & Brunch included

Spend the morning on a walking tour of Hoi An Ancient Town, famous for its well-preserved architecture, pedestrian-only streets, and the iconic Japanese Covered Bridge.

In the afternoon, participate in a traditional lantern-making class together, a perfect souvenir for couples.

  • Guided walking tour of Hoi An
  • Japanese Covered Bridge visit
  • Couples lantern-making workshop
  • Breakfast included

Take a scenic drive back to Da Nang and ride the cable car up to Ba Na Hills. Walk across the famous spectacular Golden Bridge held up by giant stone hands.

Enjoy the French Village and Fantasy Park before heading back to your hotel in Da Nang for your final night.

  • Ba Na Hills cable car ride
  • Visit to the iconic Golden Bridge
  • Exploration of the French Village
  • Breakfast included

Enjoy your final breakfast in Vietnam. Depending on your flight schedule, you may have some free time for last-minute shopping or beach relaxation before transferring to the airport for your onward journey.

  • Private airport transfer
  • Breakfast included
